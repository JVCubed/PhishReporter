Detecting a phishing email!

  • Don't trust the display name of who the email is.
  • Look but don't click.
  • Check for spelling errors.
  • Consider the salutation.
  • Is the email asking for personal information?
  • Beware of urgency.
  • Check the email signature.
  • Be carefull with attachments.
  • Don't believe everything you see
  • When in doubt, contact your SOC.

source: LogRhythm Labs
